Properties of logarithms:-
-   The Product Rule
-   The Quotient Rule
-   The Power Rule
-   The Change of the Base Rule

### The Product Rule
##### $$\log_{a}(b) + \log_{a}(c) = \log_{a}(b*c)$$

### The Quotient Rule
##### $$\log_{a}(b) - \log_{a}(c) = \log_{a}(\frac{b}{c})$$

### The Power Rule
##### $$a \cdot \log_b(c) = \log_b(c^a) $$

### The Change of Base Rule
##### $$ \log_b(a) = \frac{\log_c(a)}{\log_c(b)} $$
